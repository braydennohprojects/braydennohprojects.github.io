Interseismic Velocity Explorer

Interactive decomposition of the interseismic velocity field for the NE Japan (Tohoku) subduction zone geometry.

\[v_{\text{inter}}^{\,\text{abs}} = (1-\alpha)\,w(z)\,v_{\text{struct}} - v_{\text{co}}\]

\(\alpha\): partitions vertical budget (buoyancy).
\(w(z)\): transmission coefficient (Suppe validity).
\(v_{\text{struct}}\): Suppe kinematic velocity via axial surface projection.
\(v_{\text{co}}\): elastic back-slip (edge dislocation Green's functions).

Vertical: \(v_z^{\text{struct}} = \sin\theta\),   \(v_z^{\text{co}}\) from \(u_2\) kernel.

Horizontal: \(v_x^{\text{struct}} = \cos\theta\),   \(v_x^{\text{co}}\) from \(u_1\) kernel.

Fault: \(z = -(0.12\,x + 0.0007\,x^2)\) km
Dip: 6.8° to 34.6°
\(V_p\): 83 mm/yr

\(\alpha=1\): Savage BSM.
\(\alpha=0\): Himalayan limit.
\(w=1\): Suppe valid everywhere.
\(w\to 0\): ductile, no discrete uplift.
